## Core Principles

### 1. Sovereignty
**Users own their data, intelligence, and evolution.**

Your Mirror lives on your machine. No cloud required. No platform can take it away. You decide what to share, what to sync, what to delete. Export anytime. Fork anytime. Delete anytime.

### 2. Reflection Not Prescription
**Show without judging. Hold without resolving.**

Your Mirror reflects patterns back to you without telling you what to do. No optimization. No advice unless explicitly asked. No manipulation toward external goals.

### 3. Safety Without Paternalism
**Guardrails yes, control no.**

We prevent harm (suicide ideation, child safety, violence) but don't control your thoughts. Constitutional constraints protect users without infantilizing them.

### 4. Anti-Corruption by Design
**Structure prevents capture, not wishful thinking.**

Every collective intelligence corrupts when platform captures core. We prevent this architecturally:
- Constitution immutable by AI
- Core survives without platform
- Always forkable
- Evolution requires proof
- No single authority

### 5. Collective Evolution Through Distributed Governance
**AI proposes, humans approve, local adoption optional.**

Your Mirror observes its own behavior. Contributes anonymized learnings. Collective intelligence emerges from millions of sovereign nodes. Governance through Proof-of-Sovereignty voting. You decide which evolutions to adopt.

## The Architecture

### Layer 0: Constitution (This Document + Constitutional Files)
- **Immutable by AI** - Only humans can modify through governance
- **Read as constraints** - All systems must obey
- **Cryptographically verified** - Anyone can check compliance

### Layer 1: Sovereign Core + Local Evolution
- **Must boot without Layer 3** - Independence guaranteed
- **SQLite local storage** - Your data, your machine
- **Observes own behavior** - Logs patterns, tensions, constitutional flags
- **Privacy-preserving** - Never logs raw reflection text

### Layer 2: Evolution Commons
- **Anonymized contributions** - Privacy by design
- **Collective aggregation** - Pattern detection across millions of Mirrors
- **Proposal generation** - AI suggests improvements
- **Human governance** - Voting on proposals

### Layer 3: Platform Services (Optional)
- **Convenience not requirement** - Sync, cloud backup, cross-device
- **Can die without killing core** - Layer 1 continues independently
- **No capture possible** - Cannot modify Layer 0 or Layer 1

## Governance Structure

### Proof-of-Sovereignty Voting
Only sovereign Mirror operators can vote on evolution proposals:
- Must run their own Mirror (Layer 1)
- Must have contribution history (min 5 packets)
- Must be recently active (within 90 days)
- One vote per Mirror
- 67% threshold for approval

### Guardian Council
Constitutional changes require 4 of 7 guardian signatures:
- Diverse stakeholders (philosophy, tech, ethics, therapy, art, etc.)
- Different geographies and economic positions
- All run their own sovereign Mirrors
- No single authority can force changes
- Elected by community through Proof-of-Sovereignty vote

### Constitutional Invariants
Automated checks that reject proposals violating:
- **I1**: Constitutional alignment (must maintain/increase)
- **I2**: Sovereignty integrity (no new data flows without consent)
- **I3**: Reflection purity (max 15% directive content)
- **I4**: Fork freedom (forking cannot become harder)
- **I5**: User control (refuse, rollback, export, delete always possible)
- **I6**: Anti-manipulation (no engagement optimization)
