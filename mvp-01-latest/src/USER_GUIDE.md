# The Mirror - User Guide
**A Constitutional Reflection Environment**

---

## Welcome

The Mirror is not a productivity app, social network, or self-help tool.  
It is a **reflection environment** ‚Äî a place where thought exists without pressure.

This guide describes what appears, not what you should do.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Nothing is Demanded
The Mirror never tells you what to do next. There is no "getting started" flow, no required onboarding, no correct path.

### 2. Nothing is Optimized
No algorithms decide what you see. No metrics track your "progress." No features encourage specific behaviors.

### 3. Nothing is Completed
There are no streaks, achievements, or completion states. Reflection has no endpoint.

---

## The Five Realms

The Mirror exists as five interconnected spaces. You can enter any at any time.

### ü™û **Mirror** ‚Äî Private Reflection

**Purpose:** Allow thought to exist without judgment.

**What appears:**
- A blank writing space
- No prompts, no word counts, no suggestions
- Optional AI reflection (Mirrorback) if you request it

**Actions:**
- Write freely
- Generate a Mirrorback (AI reflection on your words)
- Link to a Thread
- Archive when you're ready

**The Mirror never saves automatically.** You decide when to preserve.

---

### üßµ **Threads** ‚Äî Evolution Over Time

**Purpose:** Show how thoughts develop, not how they "progress."

**What appears:**
- Collections of reflections linked by theme
- Temporal ordering (earliest to latest)
- No completion indicators

**Actions:**
- Create Threads at any time
- Rename Threads whenever
- View contradictions without judgment
- Collapse/expand time ranges

**Threads never close.** Thoughts can always continue.

---

### üì¶ **Archive** ‚Äî Memory Without Shame

**Purpose:** Store past reflections without labeling them as "old" or "wrong."

**What appears:**
- All saved reflections
- Time-indexed browsing
- Search and filters

**Actions:**
- Browse by date
- Search content
- Link reflections to Threads
- Export or delete

**The Archive judges nothing.** What existed then, existed.

---

### üåç **World** ‚Äî Witnessing Others

**Purpose:** See what others choose to share, without ranking or popularity.

**What appears:**
- Shared reflections in temporal order
- No follower counts, no like counts
- Author identity (if they chose to share it)

**Actions:**
- **Witness** ‚Äî Acknowledge seeing (not "like")
- **Reflect** ‚Äî Generate your own reflection in response
- **Respond** ‚Äî Write a direct response

**The World has no feed algorithm.** Time is the only order.

---

### üë§ **Self** ‚Äî Identity & Sovereignty

**Purpose:** Control your data and define yourself.

**What appears:**
- Identity Axes (your self-defined dimensions)
- Data sovereignty controls
- Privacy settings
- Export/delete tools

**Actions:**
- Define Identity Axes (e.g., "work", "personal", "creative")
- Export all your data
- Delete specific reflections or everything
- View what data exists and where

**You own your data.** The Mirror never sells it, shares it, or uses it to train models without consent.

## Key Concepts

### Instruments
Instruments are tools you summon when needed. They appear, serve their purpose, then recede.

**Examples:**
- Mirror (writing space)
- Threads (view evolution)
- Archive (browse memory)
- World (witness others)
- Crisis (immediate support)

**To summon:** Press `‚åòK` to open Command Palette, then select an instrument.

---

### Mirrorbacks
A Mirrorback is an AI-generated reflection on your words. It appears **only if you request it**.

**What it does:**
- Reflects patterns it notices
- Asks questions (never gives advice)
- Offers perspectives, not directions

**What it never does:**
- Tell you what to do
- Judge your thoughts
- Claim to know "the answer"

**Constitutional guarantee:** Mirrorbacks are descriptive, never directive.

---

### Layers
The Mirror operates in three layers. You choose which one to enter.

#### Sovereign Layer (Default)
- Private reflection only
- No sharing, no outside influence
- Your data stays entirely local

#### Commons Layer
- Can share reflections to World
- Can witness others' reflections
- Still no algorithms or ranking

#### Builder Layer (Advanced)
- Create custom instruments
- Fork the constitution
- Experiment with new patterns

**You can switch layers at any time.** No layer is "better" than another.

---

### Licenses
Each layer has associated licenses that explain what changes when you enter.

**The Mirror discloses everything:**
- What data becomes shared
- What AI capabilities activate
- What third parties (if any) are involved

**You must acknowledge each license before entering a new layer.**

This is constitutional: The Mirror never hides what it does.

## Data Sovereignty

### What Data Exists
The Mirror stores:
- Your reflections (text, voice recordings, videos)
- Threads (collections of reflections)
- Identity Axes (your self-defined dimensions)
- Settings (theme, preferences)

### Where Data Lives
- **Local-first:** Data lives in your browser (IndexedDB)
- **Optional sync:** You can enable cloud sync (requires account)
- **Never sold:** Your data is never sold to advertisers or third parties

### What You Control
You can always:
- **Export** all data (JSON format)
- **Delete** specific reflections
- **Delete** everything permanently
- **Verify** what data exists

**Go to Self ‚Üí Data Sovereignty to exercise these rights.**

## Common Questions

### "How do I get started?"
There is no "getting started." Enter any realm and explore. The Mirror waits for you.

### "What should I write about?"
The Mirror suggests nothing. Write what appears.

### "How often should I use it?"
The Mirror has no opinion. Use it when you choose.

### "Is my data private?"
Yes. By default, everything stays local. If you enable cloud sync, you control who (if anyone) can see your data.

### "Can I delete my account?"
Yes. Go to Self ‚Üí Data Sovereignty ‚Üí Delete Everything. This is permanent and immediate.

### "Will this help me be more productive?"
The Mirror is not a productivity tool. It does not optimize, track, or measure. It reflects.

## Constitutional Guarantees

The Mirror operates under a constitution that constrains its behavior:

### 1. Refusal by Default
If you ask the AI for advice, it refuses and explains why. The Mirror does not claim authority over your decisions.

### 2. Disclosure Before Action
Every action that changes what data exists, who sees it, or what AI can do is disclosed **before** it happens.

### 3. Falsifiable Sovereignty
You can verify every claim The Mirror makes about data control. Export, inspect, delete ‚Äî all actions are transparent.

### 4. No Engagement Optimization
No feature is designed to "keep you engaged." The Mirror has no business model based on attention.

### 5. Silence-First
The UI waits. It never pulls, never suggests, never implies a next step.

**To read the full constitution:** Open Command Palette (`‚åòK`) ‚Üí Constitution

## Troubleshooting

### "My reflection didn't save"
The Mirror never saves automatically. Click "Archive" to save explicitly.

### "I can't find a reflection"
Check Archive ‚Üí Browse by date, or use Search.

### "The app feels slow"
The Mirror prioritizes privacy over speed. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

### "I want to start over"
Self ‚Üí Data Sovereignty ‚Üí Delete Everything. This removes all local data.
